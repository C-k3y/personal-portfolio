# 🤱 MamaCare AI

**AI-Powered Maternal Healthcare Platform for Smarter Pregnancy Monitoring and Early Risk Detection**

## Overview

MamaCare AI is an intelligent maternal healthcare platform designed to improve pregnancy care through Artificial Intelligence, predictive analytics, and digital health technologies. The platform helps expectant mothers monitor their pregnancy, receive personalized health recommendations, and identify potential complications early, while enabling healthcare providers to remotely monitor patients and intervene when necessary.

The goal is to make quality maternal healthcare more accessible, especially in underserved communities, by combining AI-driven insights with secure digital health services.

## Problem Statement

Maternal mortality remains a major challenge in many developing countries due to delayed diagnosis of pregnancy complications, limited access to healthcare facilities, missed antenatal appointments, and inadequate continuous monitoring. Existing pregnancy applications often provide general information but lack intelligent risk prediction and personalized healthcare support.

MamaCare AI addresses these challenges by offering real-time pregnancy monitoring, AI-assisted risk assessment, and direct communication between mothers and healthcare providers.
